We Care About Your Safety

Your safety is our top priority. At Explore Bandung, we are committed to providing safe and enjoyable travel experiences. Our team follows strict safety protocols to ensure your trip is both fun and secure.

Founded in 2024, Explore Bandung was born out of a love for travel and a deep appreciation for the unique culture, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant energy of Bandung. We recognized a need for seamless and hassle-free travel solutions, and our goal is to make exploring Bandung as enjoyable and effortless as possible for both locals and tourists alike.

We Adhere to the Highest Safety Standards:

  • Comprehensive safety measures at all destinations
  • Trained and experienced guides
  • Regular health and safety checks
  • Easy access to emergency services
  • Customer support throughout your journey

Health and Safety Protocols

  • Regular sanitation of all facilities
  • Social distancing measures in place
  • Health screenings for staff
  • Emergency protocols for any situation
  • Compliance with local regulations

Experienced Guides

  • Highly trained tour guides
  • Knowledgeable about local attractions
  • Trained in first aid and emergency response
  • Ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience
  • Available to answer any questions

Emergency Services

  • Access to local emergency contacts
  • First aid kits available
  • Real-time updates on local conditions
  • Support from our customer service team
  • 24/7 assistance for urgent needs
